la ranita que no sabía cantar

la ranita que no sabía cantar is a children’s story written by Diego and Baltazar Arismendi. it tells the adventure of Paolita the frog and her family as they search for a cure for her terrible singing voice. the story interacts with children, encouraging them to draw and imagine scenes from the tale, making them the main protagonists and creators of the illustrations.

made in 2022
