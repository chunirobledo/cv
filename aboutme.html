about me

shaped by both art and music, i’ve been passionate about creating in every form since i was a child — drawing, editing, designing, and experimenting with different mediums.

at 18, i began my studies at the National University of Lanús, pursuing a degree in design and visual communication, where i learned how to give real meaning and purpose to my ideas. my goal is clear: to bring other people’s ideas to life with a unique identity — merging them with my own creative vision.
